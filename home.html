Tour Package Itinerary

Italy is a land of wonders waiting to be discovered. With our meticulously crafted tour package, you'll have the opportunity to immerse yourself in the beauty, culture, and rich history of this enchanting country.

Day 1 - Naples & Pompeii:

Experience the ancient ruins of Pompeii and the vibrant city of Naples, soaking in their rich history and captivating sights.

1

Day 2 - Rome:

Immerse yourself in the wonders of Rome, exploring iconic landmarks like the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill.

2

Day 3 - Pisa & Florence:

Discover the Leaning Tower of Pisa and indulge in the artistic treasures of Florence, a city steeped in Renaissance history.

3

Day 4 - Free Day:

Enjoy a leisure day to explore at your own pace or revisit your favorite spots in Venice

4

Day 5 - Venice, Murano & Burano:

Experience the charm of Venice, visit Murano's glass factories, and explore the colorful island of Burano.

5

Day 6 - Verona:

Step into Verona's romantic ambiance, explore historical sites, and visit Arena di Verona.

6

Day 7 - Milan, Departure to Manila:

Conclude your journey in Milan, the fashion capital, before departing for your next adventure back home.
