Booking

  • To secure your reservation, a 50% deposit of the total tour price is required.
  • The remaining balance must be paid in full at least 30 days prior to the tour's start date.

Cancellation and Refund

    Cancellation
    - Cancellation requests must be submitted at least two weeks before the scheduled tour date.
    - In cases of medical issues or severe illness, tour rebooking may be allowed with a slight additional charge.
    - No-shows are not eligible for any refund.
    - Failure to arrive at the designated tour meeting point on time will result in a non-refundable cancellation on the spot.
    Refund
    - If a cancellation is made at least two weeks prior to the tour, a refund may be granted. The exact refund percentage may vary (please check with our team).
    - Payment options include credit cards and cash.
